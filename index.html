Meet the Designer

Hello! I'm Grace Marcheschi, an aspiring interior designer passionate about turning rooms into restful, functional spaces. I am endlessly inspired by the way spaces make us feel. This site is a personal curation of interiors I love: soft light, layered textures, and pretty details. I believe good design doesn't shout; it whispers, lingers, and gently shapes the way we live. Here, I share the spaces, ideas, and color stories that move me, in hopes they'll move you as well.
